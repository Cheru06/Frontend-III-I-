My Interests

Outside of academics and projects, I have several strong interests that motivate me and guide my future goals:

Quantum Physics & Astrophysics

I am deeply fascinated by quantum mechanics and astrophysics. My dream is to contribute to space research and possibly become an astrophysicist or astronaut. Understanding the universe at both microscopic and cosmic levels excites me every day.

Technology & Innovation

I enjoy keeping up with the latest trends in AI, quantum computing, and supercomputers. These technologies are not just fascinating but also have the potential to solve complex problems in weather prediction, disaster management, and communication systems.

Fitness & Discipline

Fitness and discipline play a big role in my daily routine. I walk regularly, plan to include calisthenics, and focus on balanced nutrition. I also believe in accountability, creating groups and systems to keep track of progress.
