About Us

Cheru.tech is an innovative IT solutions company dedicated to empowering businesses through cutting‑edge technology and forward‑thinking strategy. Founded in 2016 in Bangalore, Cheru.tech began as a small team of passionate software engineers who believed in the transformative power of digital solutions. Over the years, it has grown into a full‑spectrum technology partner, serving clients across sectors such as finance, healthcare, education, and e‑commerce. At the heart of Cheru.tech lies a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.Cheru.tech prides itself on a team of multidisciplinary experts—developers, system architects, UX/UI designers, data scientists, and project managers—who bring together diverse skills and perspectives. Together, they drive the company’s vision: to simplify complexity, enable growth, and create lasting impact through technology.
